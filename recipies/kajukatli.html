Kaju Katli

Indian Origin Sweet dish made of chest nuts.

Ingridients

  1. Chestnuts 2.5 cups
  2. Sugar 3/4 cups
  3. Water 3/4 cups
  4. Clarified butter (Ghee) 1 tsp

Steps

  • Grind chestnuts to form powder. Do not grind too much otherwise it will become oily
  • Filter out the powder, you will be left with roughly 2 cups of powder
  • Put pan on medium heat
  • Add water and sugar
  • melt sugar. We don't want sugar to caramalise
  • Add the powder and keep stiring it until it become paste
  • Add 1 tsp ghee
  • Add this point, it should be able to form loose ball or it should be sticky dough
  • pull out and cool at room temp to be able to handle it bare hand. Do not put in fridge
  • After cooling, layer it on butter paper and smoothen the top with roller
  • Let it cool at room temperature. Do not put in fridge
  • Cut and serve
